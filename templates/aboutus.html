About this project

The web application, Your Stock Portfolio, is my final project in the course CS50x, Introduction to Computer Science.
I decided to make a web application for two reasons.

  1. I wanted to learn it better.
  2. Developing front-end was hardest part for me to grasp during the course.
In hindsight, developing a web application one on my own from scratch brought with it a lot of frustration. However I did learn and I look forward to learning more in this field in the future!
The source code for this project is avalible on github

Your Stock Portfolio

The idea of the project came to me by looking at my own stock portfolio and trying to determine the distribution of different sectors. At the time, the application I used did not have a possibility to show the distribution in a diagram, thus I decided to create an application for this cause. This application, Your Stock Portfolio, also gives you the possibility to try out different set-ups for your portfolio. It gives you the possibility to properly analyze the distribution of the portfolio before buying.
Your Stock Portfolio uses data from IEx Cloud.

I studied the CS50x via edx.org.
